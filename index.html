Built by a pastor and battle tested.

I'm Ryan Hayden, the guy behind Simple Church Tools. I'm not just a web developer, I've been the pastor of a very normal and traditional church in Mattoon, IL since 2012. Prior to that, I served as an assistant pastor, school teacher, children's pastor, Christian camp director and song leader. I Love churches and want to help them any way I can.

My passion is to build simple tools that can help smaller churches grow and do what they do better. If your church has a large staff, a large budget, and a smoke machine on the stage - you can probably afford to use more complicated and expensive tools. But if one or two people are trying to do almost EVERYTHING around your church - my tools will help you save time, be consistent, and look good.

I built this tool with the church where I serve as pastor in mind. I was sick of thumbing though a hymn book, guessing how long it's been since we sung that song. I wanted my Assistant Pastor, pianists, and musicians to be on the same page.
I sincerely hope that it is a blessing to your church.

Ryan Hayden - Pastor, Bible Baptist Church of Mattoon, IL.
